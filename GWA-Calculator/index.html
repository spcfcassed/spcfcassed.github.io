We use the general formula for Weighted Average:

\(W=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_i X_i}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_i}\)

  • \(W\) = weighted average
  • \(n\) = number of terms (in this case, number of your subjects)
  • \(w_i\) = weights applied to \(X\) values (or your units)
  • \(X_i\) = data values to be averaged (you guessed it; its your grades)
